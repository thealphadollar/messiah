This project is still in its infancy. Inspite of our relentless efforts, our product is still short of our ambition, due to lack of time. Developers are free to contribute to this open-source project, in whichever form they wish.
We have few ideas in mind, which if implemented, will go a long way in improving the scenario and usability of the app.
They are :

Implement an SMS service, using the Twilio API, for assisting users remotely, or for slow internet connections.

Implement a system, where users can add their family members, mark them safe, and forward messages to all of them.

Design an interactive platform between the relief providers and the users, to communicate effectively, and quickly.

Feel free to contact us for any issues, or for feature improvements.
