ADD FAMILY MEMBERS

What is it?
  • The SMS services is made to help you connect with your family members at times of distress, even without internet. You can communicate with your near and dear ones with a simple message, from anywhere you want.
  • You can add new phone numbers of your family, to whom you want to send your message.
  • You can then type your message content, and choose the message recipients from the list of already added numbers.
Due to time and finance constraints, only the front-end is made. Only the prototype is left to be activated.
